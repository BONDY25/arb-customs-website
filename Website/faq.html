FAQ

Q: What do you do exactly?

A: At ARB Customs, we provide solderless wiring solutions for electric guitars, allowing you to upgrade, modify, or refresh your guitar's electronics without needing a soldering iron. We also plan to offer individual kit parts, such as pre-wired switches, pots, and output jacks, so you can mix, match, or replace components to suit your needs.



Q: Why don't you sell a kit for my guitar?

A: Chances are, your guitar is already on our radar. We have a long list of circuits, kits, and solutions in development, and it's only a matter of time before we release something for your model! The process from concept to finished product takes time and effort as we focus on delivering the highest quality at a cost-effective price. We encourage customers to show interest in specific products by using our "Have Your Say" page, where you can vote on potential future kits and products.



Q: Do you take custom orders?

A: Yes and no. Custom orders are possible, but the cost of sourcing materials, developing the circuit, and prototyping would likely result in a higher price than we'd prefer for our customers. If you're really interested, please contact us, but we recommend using our pre-wired components, which you can assemble yourself or have us part-assemble for you.



Q: Why solderless?

A: Soldering isn't for everyone, but music is! Our solderless kits allow musicians to avoid the hassle of figuring out soldering techniques, from iron temperature to choosing the right solder. Not everyone has the tools or experience to create high-quality wiring, and that's okay. Instead of spending £200 on a professional solder setup for one guitar, you could purchase a "Plug & Play" kit from us for £40-£60.



Q: How easy are your kits to install?

A: Our kits range from very easy to a little fiddly. We've taken care of most of the complicated work. The trickiest part is usually connecting the correct wires to the right terminals, but we provide extensive guides and are always happy to assist if you get stuck.



Q: Do you ship internationally?

A: No, unfortunately, we do not offer international shipping at this time. As a small operation, the cost of shipping internationally isn't practical for us.



Q: Can I purchase directly from your website?

A: Currently, purchases can only be made through our affiliated platforms, eBay and Etsy.



Q: Do you accept backorders?

A: No, we don't accept backorders at this time. Since we are a small operation and make kits in batches with the highest-quality components, it can take time to gather materials, so we only sell what's in stock.
