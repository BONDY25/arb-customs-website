Welcome to ARB Customs!

Here at ARB Customs, we offer high-quality, handmade guitar wiring solutions that allow you to upgrade, refresh, or completely transform your guitar's electronics.

ARB Customs was founded when Aiden Bond, the creator, wanted to rewire his Les Paul without adhering to the traditional 2-volume, 2-tone control layout. In an effort to create a bespoke and versatile control scheme, Aiden began designing circuits that would offer unique tonal possibilities. After completing the project, he reflected on the challenges he faced, including the limitations of traditional soldering. Once the pickups are wired in, removing them typically requires cutting the wires or desoldering. Aiden devised a solution that allows pickups, switches, and output jack sockets to be connected to the main controls without soldering, enabling easy installation, maintenance, and modification. And thus, ARB Customs was born.
